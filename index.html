About Me:

Hello, my name is Dale Langford. I am a professional archaeologist with over seven years experience working in commercial archaeology. During this time I have had the opportunity to work on projects throughout Ontario (Canada), the United Kingdom, and the Republic of Ireland. This website has been designed to provide an overview of my archaeological career, as well as to highlight what I feel are some of the more unique artefacts and adventures I have had. Please enjoy!
